Sending Emails in Python:
---------------------------------
use smtplib and email modules for sending emails, notification
    alerts, reports and some automated task.

smtplib:
    it provides user for sending emails via SMTP (simple mail
    transfer protocol)
    connect with email servers like gmail,outlook etc..

email: helping you to build email messages
        include: subject, body, sender, receiver, attachments
