It is bucket level automatic Replication

step:

1. create 2 buckets you can create them in diffrent region or in
another account.
    source_bucket_001
    dest_bucket_001

    make sure you enable versioning in both.

2. In source Bucket --> Management --> Replication
    create rule

        choose destination bucket (dest_bucket_001)

destination bucket will replication all your source bucket data.
also select all objects or just a prefix.

Now, every new upload/update is replicaated to dest bucket not
the older one.

(Execute this later as task)
